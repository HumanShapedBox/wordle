What is Wordle?
Your challenge is to guess a five-letter word.
Wordle is a simple game that there are hardly any rules. But here you go:
You have to guess the Wordle in six goes or less
Every word you enter must be in the word list. There are more than 10,000 words in this list
A correct letter stays on board
A correct letter in the wrong place goes below board
An incorrect letter goes to the "No such letter here" list
Letters can be used more than once
Answers are never plurals
Good luck!
